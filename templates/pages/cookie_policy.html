Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 07, 2026

Education Village uses cookies to improve your user experience and ensure the platform runs smoothly.

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us remember your session and preferences.

Essential Cookies

These are necessary for you to log in and use our secure features. Without these, the site would not function correctly.

Preference Cookies

We use these to remember your language choice (Bangla/English) and your preferred study level.

How to Disable Cookies

You can disable cookies through your browser settings, but please note that some parts of Education Village may not work as expected.
